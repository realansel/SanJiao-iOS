# ICP/App 备案申诉说明（App Review Resolution Center 回复用）

> 若 Apple 审核要求提供中国大陆 ICP/App 备案号，把下方对应语言的文字粘进 App Review 的回复框。
> 口径核心：**不提供互联网信息服务 + 仅使用 Apple 系统服务**。切勿声称"完全不联网"。

---

## English

Hello, and thank you for the review.

Regarding the request for an ICP / App filing number, we would like to clarify why 青羽记账 (Quillion) does not provide an internet information service and therefore should fall outside the scope of that requirement:

Quillion is a fully on-device personal bookkeeping app.

- All user data (transactions, categories, notes) is stored and processed locally on the device using Apple's SwiftData. We operate **no servers**; the app has no backend, no user accounts, and no login.
- The app does not host, deliver, or distribute any internet content, and does not collect or transmit any user data to us or to any third party.
- The only network-related functionality relies **solely on Apple's own system services**:
  - In-App Purchase via StoreKit (a one-time unlock; Apple is the merchant of record);
  - Optional speech-to-text for voice entry — performed on-device on supported models; on older models it uses Apple's Speech Recognition service;
  - Optional iCloud backup, controlled entirely by the user's system settings.

Because the app provides no internet information service of our own and relies only on Apple's system services, we believe an ICP / App filing is not applicable. We kindly ask you to reconsider approval. If a filing is nonetheless required, please let us know and we will proceed accordingly.

Thank you for your time.

## 中文

您好，感谢审核。

关于要求提供中国大陆 ICP / App 备案号，我们说明如下，以阐明青羽记账（Quillion）不提供互联网信息服务、应不属于备案范围：

青羽记账是一款完全在设备本地运行的个人记账工具。

- 全部用户数据（账单、分类、备注）通过 Apple SwiftData 存储并处理于用户设备本地。我们**没有任何服务器**，应用无后端、无账号、无登录。
- 应用不托管、不提供、不分发任何互联网内容，也不向我方或任何第三方收集或上传用户数据。
- 涉及网络的功能**仅依赖 Apple 自身的系统服务**：
  - 通过 StoreKit 的 App 内购（一次性解锁，Apple 为收款方）；
  - 可选的语音转文字（支持的机型在设备端完成，较旧机型使用 Apple 语音识别服务）；
  - 可选的 iCloud 备份（完全由用户的系统设置控制）。

鉴于本应用不自行提供互联网信息服务、仅使用 Apple 系统服务，我们认为其不属于需要 ICP / App 备案的范围，恳请重新考虑通过。如仍需备案，请告知，我们将据此办理。

感谢您的时间。
